# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Centroid is a technical research project. Participants are expected to keep
discussion respectful, evidence-oriented, and focused on reproducible
engineering work.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and professional language.
- Critique ideas with technical evidence.
- Respect the non-claims boundary.
- Avoid sensational, anthropomorphic, or harassment-oriented framing.
- Keep private information and credentials out of issues and pull requests.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without consent
- Introducing claims of consciousness, sentience, personhood, or moral agency as
  project conclusions
- Encouraging unsafe autonomy, hidden persistence, or approval bypasses

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; reject
contributions; or restrict participation when behavior undermines project
safety, credibility, or community standards.
