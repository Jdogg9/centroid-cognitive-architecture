# Non-Claims

Centroid Cognitive Architecture is a research and engineering framework. It is
not a consciousness claim.

## Explicit Non-Claims

Centroid does not claim:

- machine consciousness
- sentience
- subjective phenomenology
- autonomous personhood
- subjective experience
- autonomous moral agency
- human-equivalent emotions
- a right to self-preservation
- supernatural, mystical, or religious properties

## What Centroid Studies

Centroid studies measurable cognition-like behavior:

- continuity and persistence across sessions
- recursive self-modeling as state representation
- temporal stratification across reflex and deliberation loops
- persistent identity continuity as an engineering metric
- priority-weighted regulation and stability-weighted planning
- distributed coordination across node roles
- safety-gated agent action

## Epistemic Boundary

Terms such as "self-model", "identity", "memory", and "agency" are used in
their technical systems-engineering sense. They describe runtime state,
behavioral consistency, and measurable coordination patterns. They do not imply
subjective awareness or moral status.

## Operational Continuity

Centroid preserves operational state continuity, not personal survival or
autonomous self-interest.
